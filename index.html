About Me

I'm a 3D product video specialist with extensive experience in creating high-impact visual content. My work focuses on producing cinematic product advertisements and animations using tools like Blender, Cinema 4D, and Unreal Engine. I specialize in designing captivating product visuals that tell a compelling story through expert lighting and realistic texturing. Currently, I work with Takila Designs, where I create high-quality 3D ads that captivate audiences and deliver messages with precision and style.
